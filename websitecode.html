A Day in the Life of a Bostonian

_____

Imagine if you are wondering in the streets of Boston. What could you possibly do in such a prestigious city? Well, think about all the lovely men and women that bring joy to our world. Imagine a world without them, and then with them again. Wow. You are mesmerized by the sheer lack of understanding derived from the egregious displays and ludicrous positionings made by our Constitution. In the Second Amendment, it is our right to carry a gun, but in this world filled with terror, how can a child who can't even buy the beer, but an assault rifle? This needs to change. I propose the Parliamation Proclamation, an advanced institute cherishing the values of the homely homes.
